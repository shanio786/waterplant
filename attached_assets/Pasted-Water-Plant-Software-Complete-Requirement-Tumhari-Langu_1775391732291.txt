Water Plant Software – Complete Requirement (Tumhari Language mein)
🟤 1. Khali Bottles System

Sab se pehle:

Hum khali bottles purchase / receive karte hain
Har type ka record hona chahiye:
Bottle Types:
500ml
1.5 Liter
5 Liter
19 Liter
System mein:
Kitni khali bottles available hain (type wise)
Kitni aaj aayi hain
Total khali stock
💧 2. Filling / Conversion System

Process:

Khali bottles ➝ Fill ho kar full bottles ban jati hain
System logic:
Khali stock kam hoga
Full stock barhega
Record hona chahiye:
Aaj kitni bottles fill hui
Kis type ki kitni fill hui
🔵 3. Full Bottles Stock

System bataye:

Kitni full bottles ready hain
Kitni sale ho chuki hain
Kitni remaining hain
🚚 4. Sales / Invoice System

Jab customer ko bottles dete hain:

System kare:
Invoice generate kare
Multiple products add ho sakte hain
Rate + quantity
Print:
A4 invoice
Thermal receipt
👥 5. Customer System

Har customer ka record:

Naam
Phone
Address
Special:
19L bottle tracking (returnable)
Customer ke paas kitni 19L bottles hain
💰 6. Accounts & Ledger System

Har customer ka full account:

Ledger mein:
Total bill
Payment ayi
Remaining balance
System bataye:
Kitna lena hai (Receivable)
Kitna aa gaya
Kitna baki hai
🔁 7. Return System (Important)
2 Types Return:
1. Product Return
Customer ne kuch bottles wapas kar di
Stock wapas add ho
Ledger adjust ho
2. 19L Bottle Return (Can System)
Customer ne khali can wapas di
Ya exchange hua (full diya, khali liya)
System track kare:
Customer ke paas kitni bottles hain
Kitni wapas aayi
📊 8. Daily Report System

Roz ka summary:

Aaj total sale
Aaj total cash receive
Aaj ka credit (udhaar)
Important:
Aaj kin customers ka balance pending hai
📈 9. Reports
Customer wise balance
Ledger history
Product wise sale
Stock report (khali + full)
🧾 10. Invoice & History
Purane bills save hon
Dubara open ho sakte hain
Re-print option (A4 + thermal)
🔄 11. Complete Workflow (Simple)
Khali bottles aayi
Fill hui → full ban gayi
Sale hui → invoice bana
Payment ayi / udhaar bana
Ledger update
Return ayi (agar hui) → adjust
🧠 Core System Logic (Important)
Stock:
Khali = Previous + Received - Filled
Full = Previous + Filled - Sold + Return
Ledger:
Balance = Total Sale - Payment - Return
